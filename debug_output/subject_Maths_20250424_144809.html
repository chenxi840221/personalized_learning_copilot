What is a quadrilateral?

A quadrilateral is a four-sided shape, where the sides are straight and meet at four vertices. The prefix “quad” means four. There are several types of quadrilaterals. Can you name them?
Duration: 3 minutes 50 seconds3m 50s

What is a triangle?

A triangle is a three-sided shape, where the sides are straight and meet at three vertices. The prefix "tri" means three. There are several types of triangles. Do you know what they are?
Duration: 3 minutes 42 seconds3m 42s

What is an angle?

An angle is formed when two straight lines, or rays, meet at a point, called a vertex. Angles are measured in degrees.
